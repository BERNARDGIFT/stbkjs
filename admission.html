Admissions

Welcome to Our Admissions Page

We are excited to welcome new students to our institution. Our admissions process is simple and structured to ensure a smooth transition.

Application Process

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your former school results (if applicable).
  3. Write an application letter.
  4. Provide letters of recommendation.
  5. Attend an interview (if required).

Important Notes

We uphold values of respect and inclusivity for all religions.

  • All submitted information must be accurate.
  • Pupils must have all required uniforms.
  • School rules and regulations must be followed.
  • Children's education is our top priority.
